Final Research Paper 
Write a 12-15-page (double-spaced) scientific report on your final project. Your final project should be an empirical report of your study from Music Cognition. It may be extended from your earlier research proposal, literature review, experiment design, and pilot results.
All reports should have the following sections: Title page, Abstract, Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion, References. Figures and tables are encouraged but are not included towards the page count. 
Introduction should have a literature review leading up to a hypothesis.
Methods should clearly describe the subjects, stimuli, and procedures of your study.
Results should describe main findings. 
Papers should generally follow the APA style (see here for guidelines).
